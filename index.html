About Me


LSTC are a Well-Established and Market Leading China Based Design & Sourcing Company specialising in the Source, Design, Manufacture & Supply of a large variety of retail quality products to include bespoke clothing, bags, accessories and apparel. We produce a large variety of bespoke designed Leather/Nylon/Polyester/PU/PVC/Paper bags and cases for retail & promotional use, Presentation Boxes and Card Packaging, along with a host of accessories such as Pro Salon Products like Scissors & Gowns to Dryers & Trimmers and Promotional Goods to include bespoke Jewelry, Bracelets, Bath Ducks, Fridge Magnets to name a few.....


In fact, over the past 17 years we have worked closely with our global clients to produce a large variety of uniquely designed products for the retail & promotional industry. With offices in Shanghai, Ningbo, Xiamen, Shenzhen and Hong Kong, China we have been able to build long term successful relationships with an extensive list of secure and reputable Suppliers and Manufacturers throughout the Globe including China, Taiwan, Japan, USA, EU and India. Due to this, we are able to offer high quality items at the lowest competitive prices. With Sales Offices operating in Newark, NJ, USA and London, UK, we have a long list of clients in the corporate, retail and promotional sector throughout Europe and the USA.
